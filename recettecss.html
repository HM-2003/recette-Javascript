Ingredients

For the Risotto Base:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced

For the Mushroom Mixture:

  • 200g button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine (optional)
  • 3-4 cups vegetable or chicken broth, kept warm

For Garnishing:

  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Extra Parmesan cheese for sprinkling
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Broth: In a saucepan, heat the vegetable or chicken broth on low.
  2. Saut√© the Vegetables: In a large skillet, heat 1 tbsp of olive oil or butter over medium heat. Add onions and cook until translucent. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Cook the Mushrooms: Add sliced mushrooms to the pan and cook until golden. Season with salt and pepper. Remove from pan and set aside.
  4. Toast the Rice: In the same skillet, add remaining olive oil or butter, then add Arborio rice. Stir constantly for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add Wine and Broth Gradually: Add white wine (if using) and stir until absorbed. Begin adding warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently until the rice is creamy and tender (18-20 minutes).
  6. Finish with Mushrooms and Cheese: Add cooked mushrooms back into the risotto, and stir in Parmesan cheese.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Season with salt and pepper as needed. Serve hot with parsley and extra Parmesan.

Enjoy your delicious, creamy risotto!
